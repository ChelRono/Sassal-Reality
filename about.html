  1. Our Story

    Sassal Reality Consult was started in the year 2017 when it was registered as a bussiness. It was moved by the passion of the founder who sought to shape the lives of the young generation.

    Sassal Reality Consult aims at creating self-awareness, personal development, emotional intelligence, and goal setting.
